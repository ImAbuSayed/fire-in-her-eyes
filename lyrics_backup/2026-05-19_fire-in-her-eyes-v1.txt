[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Nigahwaan 'ch agg laike takkdi ae mainu, feel the heat
Seene vich machdi ae hulchal saari, heart skip a beat
Main taan khada haan tere raahan vich kalla, purely yours
Teriyan adawaan ne kita mainu pagal, breakin' the doors

[Hook 2]
Hawaavan 'ch udd-di ae teri mehak sohniye, toxic scent
Main taan bhula baitha apna wajood saara, time well spent
Kaali ess raat vich tuhi disdi ae, shinin' so bright
Aaja mere kol ajj ho ja meri, ownin' the night

[Verse 1]
Takkda haan tainu jadon thehar janda waqt ni, frozen in space
Mitha jeha hasa tera luttda ae chain mera, beautiful face
Khamosh ehna raahan te chalda haan kalla main, walkin' alone
Kanch de payale wangu tutt gaya dil mera, heart of stone
Chup-chap sehi janda tere saare nakhre, takin' the pain
Tere piche rulda haan ban ke shudayi main, goin' insane
Zulfan di chhaan thalle labhda haan thikana, lookin' for rest
Main taan aazma leya apna naseeb saara, givin' my best

[Pre-Chorus 1]
Tutt ke bikharda haan tere hi ishq vich, fallin' apart
Pyaar di pyaas kade hundi na poori, thirsty heart
Neendaraan vi udd gaiyaan akhiyaan de vichon, wide awake
Main taan apne saah kitte tere hi havaale, for your sake

[Chorus 1]
Naina vichon dirdi ae nashiyaan di dhaar ni, drinkin' it in
Zehar tere ishq da chadhda ae mainu, beautiful sin
Duniya nu bhull ke main takkda haan tainu, catchin' your vibe
Rooh meri mangdi ae tera hi saath ajj, joinin' my tribe
Main taan ban baitha haan aashiq tera sacha, crazy for love
Parwaah ni karda main kisse vi badal di, skies above
Kaale ehna badalaan 'ch lishkdi ae tuhi, light of my life
Chup karke sehi janda dil utte vaar main, edge of a knife

[Verse 2]
Tire'an di cheekh utte nachda haan raatan nu, pushin' the edge
Dhokhe tere seene te wajde ne teer wangu, crossin' the ledge
Main taan nibhaye saare waade vi pakke, loyal and true
Dhuen de chhalle ban udd-da haan aasmaan 'ch, flyin' to you
Zahar warge tere bol vi main hass ke sahe, takin' the hit
Thandi eh hawa vi jalaave mere jism nu, fire is lit
Manzil di fikar nai main raste te gumm haan, losin' my way
Khed-da haan jaan te main tere layi sohniye, night and day
